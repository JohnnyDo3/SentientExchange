## üìê Logo Usage

### Logo Variations

1. **Full Color Gradient** (Primary)
   - Use on: Black backgrounds, dark photos, website headers
   - Gradient: #9333ea ‚Üí #f72585

2. **White Monochrome**
   - Use on: Dark backgrounds, photography overlays
   - Color: #ffffff

3. **Purple Monochrome**
   - Use on: White/light backgrounds, print on white paper
   - Color: #9333ea

4. **Single Color Fallback**
   - Use for: Embroidery, single-color printing
   - Color: #9333ea OR #f72585

### Logo Rules

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Keep minimum clear space (0.25x logo height on all sides)
- Use high-resolution files
- Place on appropriate contrast backgrounds
- Scale proportionally

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Rotate, stretch, or distort
- Add drop shadows or effects
- Change colors outside brand palette
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Recreate the wordmark

**Minimum Size:** 120px wide (digital) | 1 inch (print)

## üéØ Common Design Tasks

### Making a Social Media Post (1080√ó1080px)

**Template:**
1. Background: Black (#000000)
2. Add purple glow effect (optional, subtle)
3. Logo: White monochrome, top-left or bottom-right
4. Headline: 48px Bold, White or Gradient
5. Body text: 24px Regular, Gray (#9ca3af)
6. Add accent: Blue or Pink elements

### Making a Flyer/Poster

**Template:**
1. Background: Black or dark purple gradient
2. Logo: Gradient version at top
3. Headline: 72px Extra-Bold, Gradient text
4. Body: 20-24px Regular, Gray (#9ca3af)
5. CTA Button: Gradient button with 16px Semi-Bold white text
6. QR Code: White on dark background (if needed)

### Making a Business Card

**Front (3.5√ó2 inches):**
- Background: Black
- Logo: Gradient
- Name: 18px White
- Title: 14px Gray

**Back:**
- Background: Full gradient (#9333ea ‚Üí #f72585)
- Contact info: White text
- Website: Bold white

### Making a Presentation Slide

**Title Slide:**
- Background: Black
- Title: 72px Gradient
- Logo: White monochrome, bottom-right

**Content Slides:**
- Background: Black or #111827
- Headline: 48px White Bold
- Body: 24px Gray Regular
- Bullet points: Purple (#9333ea) colored

## üìù Brand Voice (Quick Version)

### Tone: Intelligent, Energetic, Premium, Accessible

**DO say:**
- "Build autonomous AI agents"
- "Discover services"
- "Pay with blockchain"
- "Start building"

**DON'T say:**
- "Revolutionary disruptive solution"
- "Simple and easy platform"
- "Sign up now" (say "Start Building" instead)

### Headlines: Active voice, present tense, benefit-focused
### Body Copy: Short paragraphs, bullet points, second person ("you")

## ‚úÖ Quick Checklist

Before finalizing any design, check:

- [ ] Using official logo files (not recreated)
- [ ] Colors match exact hex codes
- [ ] Gradient is 135¬∞ diagonal (if used)
- [ ] Text is minimum 14px (body) or 16px (buttons)
- [ ] Text contrast meets WCAG AA (use WebAIM Contrast Checker)
- [ ] Logo has clear space (0.25x height on all sides)
- [ ] Using Inter font family
- [ ] Dark backgrounds with light text (or vice versa)
- [ ] Consistent spacing (multiples of 4px)
- [ ] High-resolution exports (2x for digital, 300dpi for print)
