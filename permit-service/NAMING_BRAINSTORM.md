## Domain Extension Strategy

### .ai domains
**Best for**: PermitFlow, PermitPro, QuickPermit, PermitGenius
- Emphasizes AI technology
- Modern, tech-forward
- Higher cost ($80-150/year vs $10-15 for .com)
- Strong brand signal

### .io domains
**Best for**: Permitly, PermitKit, SwiftPermit, Permitron
- Developer/tech-friendly
- Widely accepted for SaaS
- Moderate cost ($30-50/year)
- Professional

### .com domains
**Best for**: Any name if available
- Most trusted extension
- Best for SEO
- Standard cost
- Maximum credibility with contractors

### Primary Choice: **PermitFlow.ai**

**Why:**
1. **Memorable**: Easy to remember, spell, and pronounce
2. **Professional**: Conveys expertise and smooth process
3. **Scalable**: Works for any geographic market and trade
4. **Visual Brand**: Strong metaphor (flowing water = smooth process)
5. **AI Emphasis**: .ai extension reinforces technology advantage
6. **Differentiation**: Unique in permit space
7. **SEO-friendly**: Contains "permit" keyword

**Full Branding:**
- **Name**: PermitFlow
- **Domain**: permitflow.ai (primary), permitflow.com (redirect)
- **Tagline**: "Permits that flow from start to finish"
- **Positioning**: The AI-powered permit automation platform for contractors
- **Brand Voice**: Professional, helpful, efficient, trustworthy
- **Brand Colors**:
  - Primary: Deep Blue (#1e40af) - trust, flow
  - Secondary: Bright Orange (#f97316) - energy, HVAC
  - Accent: Light Blue (#60a5fa) - technology, clarity
